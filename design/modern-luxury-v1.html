Why the seal means something

A listing here is earned, not bought.

We keep the category deliberately small. A studio appears on Wedding Gully only after it clears every step below — the reason a shortlist from us is worth more than a search anywhere else.

i

Met in person

We sit with every studio — no listing is approved from a portfolio link alone. The people behind the lens are known to us.

ii

Full weddings reviewed

We view complete, unedited galleries from recent Bengaluru weddings — not the highlight reel — to judge consistency across a real day.

iii

Couples contacted

We speak directly to past couples about delivery timelines, conduct on the day, and whether the final work matched the promise.

iv

Terms held to standard

Contracts, deliverables and turnaround are checked against our standard before a studio earns the seal — and re-verified yearly.
